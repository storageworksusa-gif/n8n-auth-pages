Privacy Policy for etheris-21keg-sns

Last Updated: September 8, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how STORAGEWORKS CO.,LTD ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our internal social media automation tool, built on the N8N platform. This tool uses Google APIs to function.

Information We Access via Google APIs

Our application interacts with Google services and accesses the following data:

  • Google Sheets: We read data from cells within a specific Google Sheet. We also write data back to this sheet to update the status of a post.
  • Google Drive: We access specific media files (images and videos) from a designated Google Drive folder on a read-only basis.

How We Use This Information

Data accessed via Google APIs is used exclusively for the tool's core functionality:

  • To retrieve scheduled content from Google Sheets for publication.
  • To download associated media files from Google Drive.
  • To update the Google Sheet with the post's publication status and live URL.

Information Sharing

We do not share any data obtained through Google APIs with any third parties. All data is processed within our secure N8N instance for internal operational purposes only.

Compliance with Google Policies

The use and transfer of information received from Google APIs by this application will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at storageworksusa@gmail.com.
