About This Application:
This is an internal automation workflow built on the N8N platform. Its purpose is to automate the process of publishing social media content.

The workflow functions by reading pre-approved content and scheduling information from a designated Google Sheet. For posts containing media, it retrieves image or video files from a specific Google Drive folder. This data is used solely to construct and publish posts to our official social media channels.

This tool is for internal operational use only to improve our content management efficiency and is not a public-facing application.
